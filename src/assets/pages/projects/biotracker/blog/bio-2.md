# BLOG 2 (Manufacturing and Assembly)

---

Beginning the printing consisted of many failed attempts, playing with different printer settings like wall thickness, layer height, speed, ect. All to find the perfect settings for the project. Once printed each piece was sanded down and cleaned for dry fitting, then glued using a special plastic glue (JB-Weld), then held together by many clamps and fasteners to keep the components secure. Once the glue sets, more cleanup was required such as removing exccess glue and sanding rough areas.
